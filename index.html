Yaga Gauganek

Fairy tale

My Name is Galganek and in Polish slang it means a cloud. In human word there is no such name so I use it as my surname. My fisrt name in Yaga. I was born on a cloud. Pink and fluffy. One day, out of blue (hehe - pun not intended) I was accidentally kidnapped by Stork, William. His job was to deliver babies, but that day he got lost. He nicked me from the sky and I become a human girl. I lived in tiny house, called the Nutshell. One day I met Saint Herman Fro and he banished me to The Dark Forest. Although I wasn't alone there. I had two friends beside me. All of them were plush toys, who could talk. First was elephant-shaped pillow, called Theo; second was flower patterned seal - Foka Hoka.
